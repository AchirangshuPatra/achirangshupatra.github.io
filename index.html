About Me

I am a Ph.D. scholar in Mechatronics Engineering at C.V. Raman Global University, specializing in Industrial Automation, AI/ML applications, and Industry 4.0 solutions. With extensive experience in PLC programming, HMI design, SCADA systems, and industrial robotics, I am passionate about driving innovation in smart factory implementations.

Currently serving as an Assistant Professor in Electrical Engineering, I mentor students in WorldSkills competitions and lead the Mitsubishi Electric Authorized Training Center. My research focuses on predictive maintenance, digital twins, and low-cost smart factory implementations, combining my expertise in automation technologies with cutting-edge AI/ML applications.
